an artist that has helped redefine and transcend the urbano genre thanks to his artistic vision and passion.

C. Tangana is a Spanish rap, trap, latin pop and reggaeton artist. His real name is Antón Álvarez Alfaro, and he was born in Madrid on July 16, 1990. Under the nickname El Madrileño, C. Tangana was responsible for bringing “the European wave” to the Latin music scene, thanks to global hits such as “Mala Mujer”, “Llorando en la Limo”, “Bien Duro” or his collaboration with Becky G to do “Booty.”
